Invisible Grill FAQs

Common questions about our invisible grills

Our invisible grills use high-tensile stainless steel cables that can withstand over 150kg of pressure per cable. The entire system is engineered to provide maximum safety while maintaining minimal visibility.

While not completely invisible, our grills are designed to be as unobtrusive as possible. From just a few feet away, they become nearly invisible, especially against outdoor backgrounds. The thin stainless steel cables (2mm or 3mm) blend seamlessly with most views.

Invisible grills provide equivalent or better safety than traditional grills while offering these advantages: unobstructed views, modern aesthetic, no painting required, and easier window cleaning. They're also more resistant to rust and corrosion.

We offer special child-safe locking mechanisms that prevent children from opening the grills without adult supervision. These can be added to any of our invisible grill installations.

With proper installation, our invisible grills typically last 10-15 years with minimal maintenance. The stainless steel cables and frames are resistant to rust and weathering.
